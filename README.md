  # UMOCloud Privacy Policy 

</div>

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

I. Scope of Application

This privacy policy applies to the cloud phone products and related services provided by UMO Cloud, including but not limited to software applications, websites, etc.

II. Legal Compliance

This privacy policy complies with the laws and regulations of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region and refers to internationally recognized data protection principles and best practices.

III. Collection of Personal Information

(1) Account Registration

When you register for a UMO Cloud account, we collect your registration information, including name, registered phone number, registered email, etc., to confirm your identity as a user of UMO Cloud's cloud phone      service. If you refuse to provide this information, we will be unable to offer you account registration services.

(2) Product Usage

When using the cloud phone service, our servers automatically collect and aggregate relevant information, including but not limited to:

- Your UMO Cloud account information;
- Access IP addresses for network management and security protection;
- Device information such as operating system and browser type to optimize service compatibility;
- Cloud phone usage details, such as running applications and usage duration, to enhance your service experience.

(3) Customer Support

When contacting our customer support team via phone, email, or other communication channels, you may provide personal or account information for technical support or query resolution.

(4) App Push Services

To offer more personalized and timely notifications, our app may integrate third-party push services. This allows us to send device notifications about new content, promotions, and service updates.
Data Collection

When using push services, we may collect:


- Device identifiers (IMEI, IDFA, Android ID) to recognize your device and customize push content;
- App usage data, including launch frequency and duration, to optimize push effectiveness;
- User feedback on push notifications, such as clicks and dismissals, to improve our services.

IV. Use of Personal Information

Collected information is used for lawful business purposes:


- Providing cloud phone products and services;
- Processing payments and financial matters;
- Notifying users about service updates, promotions, and other relevant information;
- Responding to user inquiries and improving products/services based on feedback;
- Conducting IP geolocation and fraud prevention;
- Verifying user account ownership and assisting technical support;
- Understanding user interaction with services to optimize performance;
- Preventing security breaches and abuse;
- Delivering and enhancing push services;
- Analyzing user behavior to improve products and services;
- Conducting marketing and promotional activities.

V. Disclosure of Personal Information

We prioritize user data protection and privacy. We won't disclose personal information to third parties without explicit user consent, except in the following cases:


- Responding to lawful requests from law enforcement and government agencies;
- Conducting personal information security audits;
- Protecting users from fraud and serious harm;
- Complying with Hong Kong Special Administrative Region laws and regulations;
- Sharing information with push service providers for service delivery and analysis.

VI. User Control over Personal Information

You can view account details like name and registered email via the personal settings interface, and edit information such as registered email, nickname, and password.
For security reasons, we don't allow users to view, modify, or delete data like login IP, operating system, and browser type.

VII. User Data Deletion

We take the protection of users’ personal data very seriously. Users have the right to request the deletion of their personal data at any time. The specific methods are as follows:

(1) Method for Submitting a Deletion Request

Users may delete their personal data by deleting their account within the application.

Account deletion page:

Please send the registration information to this email address:deven@umocloud.com

(2) Scope of Data Deletion

After verifying the user’s identity, we will delete or anonymize the following data, as applicable, in accordance with applicable laws and business requirements:

User account information

Personal identification information associated with the account

Usage records and related log data

Authorization data obtained from third-party platforms (including Facebook)

Note: If certain data must be retained due to legal, financial, or security compliance requirements, such data will be retained only for the legally required period and will not be used for any other purpose.

(3) Processing Time

We will process and complete the user’s data deletion request within a reasonable period, typically no longer than 30 days, and will provide feedback using the contact information submitted by the user.

(4) Facebook Platform Data

If a user logs in via Facebook or uses features related to Facebook, the data deletion request will also apply to the relevant user data obtained from the Facebook platform, in compliance with Facebook Platform Policies.

VIII.Logs

When accessing our website, we may collect your IP address to diagnose server issues and manage the site. IP addresses are used solely for this purpose, and website access logs may be retained for six months.

IX.Use of Cookies

We use Cookies on our website for session management and to save settings/preferences. Third-party Cookies may also be used to collect visitor statistics and measure marketing campaign effectiveness. You can refuse third-party Cookies by adjusting your browser settings.

X.Children's Privacy

We cannot guarantee that children won't access our website or use our cloud phone services without parental consent. We advise parents to read this privacy policy before allowing their children to use our services and      ensure proper guidance during use.

XI.Push Service Providers

Our push services are provided by third parties such as MobPush. Review  their privacy policies to understand how your personal information is handled.

(1) User Control

Manage push services via device settings, including enabling or disabling notifications. You can also adjust push preferences through the app settings interface.

(2) Push Service Providers

We share collected information with push service providers to enable service delivery and necessary data analysis. Information is only disclosed in the following circumstances:

- Obtaining explicit user consent;
- Complying with legal and regulatory requirements;
- Protecting our legitimate rights and interests or public interest.

XII.International Data Transfer

As our company is based in the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region, collected personal information may be stored in Hong Kong or transferred to other regions. We take necessary measures to ensure data transfer security and compliance with Hong Kong laws. Within the scope of applicable laws, we obtain valid user consent and ensure adequate  protection of personal information by the recipient.

We prioritize user information security and implement strict measures to prevent unauthorized access, use, disclosure, or destruction of data. These include:

- Using firewalls and encryption technologies;
- Restricting physical access to data storage centers;
- Contracting with partners to ensure compliance with data processing requirements.
We also conduct regular security audits and risk assessments to safeguard personal information.
